How to Make Wudu (Ablution)

Before we pray, we must be clean! Wudu means washing hands, mouth, nose, face, arms, wiping the head, and washing the feet. It makes us ready to talk to Allah!

The Five Daily Prayers

Muslims pray 5 times dialy: Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha. These prayers connect us to Allah and help us stay close to Him.

Other Fiqh Topics

  • Ghusl (Full Body Wash): Needed after certain actions to be fully clean for prayer.
  • Salah Times: Learning when each prayer time starts and ends.
  • Clean Clothes and Place: Prayer must be done in clean clothes and a clean place.
  • Intention (Niyyah): Always begin prayer or worship with a sincere intention in your heart.
